Returns & Exchanges

Our Policy

Regular Items

We want you to be satisfied & happy. If you are not completely happy with your purchase(s), we will gladly refund your purchases within 30 days of receipt. However, returns outside of this period are not accepted at our discretion.

Defective or Incorrect merchandise(s)

Please see the policy for regular items.

Please note, for all refunds or exchanges, items must HAVE NOT BEEN WORN, ALTERED OR WASHED, and with ALL tags attached. Any designer packaging such as boxes, dust bags, authenticity cards and tags are considered part of the product. Merchandises returned not in its original state may result in delays and may not qualify for a refund. Ifchic reserves the rights to request photographic support for defective or incorrect merchandise before authorizing a return. Ifchic reserves the rights to not accept any merchandise(s) that does not follow the terms and conditions of the return policy. Once returned items are received, full refunds will be credited to your original method of payment.

Sale Items

All SALE purchases are not subject to return or exchange.

Items Purchased with ifchic coins

Please note that no refunds or exchanges for purchases made with ifchic coins.

Returning & Exchanging Your Purchase(s)

Making a return or exchange is easy, follow the steps below:

  • Simply sign in to your account and click on Order History, then select the order number containing the items you would like to return.
  • An email containing returning number will be sent to you follow by the email for return label.
  • Cover or remove any original mailing address label or carrier barcodes from the package.
  • Print out the return label from the email and affix the shipping label to your package.
  • Drop off your package at any UPS station. Please remember to request for a proof of posting certificate and keep it safe for your reference. Without this, we cannot refund any money if we do not receive your parcel.
  • You will receive a payment refund confirmation email once your return has been completed.

Processing time

Please note that you will be notified via email to the address provided on your account once the refund has been processed.
Please allow up to 4 weeks to process your return.
