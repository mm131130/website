ABOUT US

Our inspiration comes from within our own team. We are a group of young professional women from all walks of life. Some of us are single and enjoy a more cosmopolitan lifestyle; some of us are working mothers whose schedules are dictated by our little ones; some of us simply independent women working hard to make it in a man’s world. One thing we all have in common is a love for fashion (and cupcakes). We don’t get many moments to indulge ourselves. Sometimes though we need a little more than freshly brewed morning coffee and bedtime facials; sometimes we need to treat ourselves to a new outfit. With busy schedules and limited time, we loved the convenience shopping online provided but wanted a website that truly understands what it is like being a modern women, and so IFCHIC was born.

IFCHIC is an online fashion site tailored for contemporary women and their busy lifestyles. We handpick our products from a sea of brilliant designers and only select the styles best suited for our active lifestyles. If you are like us, with endless things to do and never enough time, and you still have a love for fashion we think you will like what we have in store for you.
