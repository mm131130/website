ifchic coins FAQ

How do ifchic coins work?

An ifchic coins is equivalent to $1 USD. A $2 ifchic coins is equivalent to $2 USD. Each coin will show the value on the coin itself. Visit out website daily, and you can collect up to $6 per day for 30 days. Save up to $180 on your entire purchase. Spend your coins exclusively on #IFCHIC24 (24th of every month, 24 hours only).

Where can I find ifchic coins?

ifchic coins can be collected on different pages of our website. To collect ifchic coins, simply click on the coin and it's collected.

How many can I collect in one day?

You can collect up to $6 in ifchic coins per day.

Do ifchic coins expire?

All unused ifchic coins will expire by the end of our monthly #IFCHIC24 event at 11:59pm ET on the 24th of every month.

Can I use ifchic coins to pay for items that are not a whole dollar?

No, ifchic coins can only be paid in $1 increments. If your order total is $100.25, you can spend up to $100 ifchic coins on the order, but must cover the remainin $0.25 yourself.

Can I use ifchic coins with a promotional code?

ifchic coins cannot be combined with a promotional code on the same purchase.

Can I use ifchic coins on sale items?

Yes, you can use ifchic coins for items already on sale.

Can I save ifchic coins for more than a month?

No, ifchic coins can only be used on the 24th of every month (#IFCHIC24 event). All unspent coins will expire by the end of our monthly #IFCHIC24 event at 11:59pm ET.

Can I use ifchic coins any time any day?

ifchic coins can only be used between 12:01am ET to 11:59pm ET on the 24th day of each month, also referred to as #IFCHIC24.

Can ifchic coins be transferred to another member's account?

No, ifchic coins must be used only by the member whom originally signed up the account and collected the coins.

Can ifchic coins be used on multiple items?

Yes, on the 24th day of the month, ifchic coins are used towards your entire purchase, regardless of the number of items.

Can I return or receive a refund if I use ifchic coins on my order?

No refunds or exchanges on any orders made using ifchic coins.

Are coins transferable?

No. ifchic coins are not transferable between accounts.

Can I exchange ifchic coins for cash or credit?

No. ifchic coins cannot be exchanged for money or credit. They may only be used towards a purchase on the 24th of every month.

Can I use coins towards shipping?

ifchic provides free U.S. continental shipping for all order over $75. However, ifchic coins cannot be used towards express shipping or other shipping fees.
