It’s simple! Just log into your ifchic account, go to your Invite-a-Friend section and select 3 of your best friends to invite to ifchic.com. For each friend that registers both of you will receive $20 ifchic coins.

STEP 2

Enter up to 3 separate friend emails. Also share through social media to increase your chances of getting 3 friends to register.

STEP 3

Check your “Invite-a-Friend” section regularly to keep track of your referrals each month and how much you earned!
