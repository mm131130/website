Shipping charges are calculated based on the subtotal after Ifchic Coins & promo codes have been applied.

Ifchic Coins cannot be used for shipping charges.

All packages are shipped via UPS or USPS, please allow 1-2 business days for processing. When your order is ready to ship, it will take up to 5-7 business days for delivery, shipping time may vary due to availability of merchandise.

Need it faster? You may also choose to upgrade to Expedited Delivery for an additional charge (see chart above). Oversized and overweight packages may incur additional charges and services restrictions. At this time, we are unable to deliver to US territories, APO/FPO addresses, as well as P.O. Boxes.

Tracking Your Order

If you want to track your order shipment via UPS/USPS, you can do so in the following way: Refer to your shipping confirmation email for the UPS/USPS tracking number; then simply enter your tracking number on the UPS website (www.ups.com) or USPS website (www.usps.com) to track your order shipment.

Out of Stock Items

Although we make every effort to keep sufficient stock of items listed on our site, occasionally we do sell out of certain products. If we are out of stock on an item that you've ordered, we will notify you via email. Any changes will be reflected in your order total as well as your shipping confirmation.
