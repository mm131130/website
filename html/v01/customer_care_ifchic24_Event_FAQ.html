#ifchic24 Event FAQ

What is #ifchic24?

#ifchic24 is an exclusive 24 hour promotional event on the 24th of every month at ifchic.com. Explore contemporary designers and emerging labels at an amazing price on this day. Redeem up to $180 in ifchic coins on your entire purchase at checkout, including sale items! Shop our buyer's top picks with up to 50% off only on #ifchic24. Don't miss out, and start collecting ifchic coins today!

Why 24?

#ifchic24 happens on the 24th of every month for 24 hours! This lucky number is easy to remember. Come visit on the 24th and and shop for 24 hours!

What happens once the event is over?

Starting on the 25th of each month, you can start collecting ifchic coins again for the next #ifchic24.

Are there any restrictions to #ifchic24?

ifchic coins cannot be used together with any other promotional codes.

Are your products authentic?

We guarantee all items on ifchic.com are authentic. We work directly with the original designer brands and are stored at our secure warehouse location in California.

Will I be reminded of #ifchic24?

Information regarding #ifchic24 is always posted on ifchic.com. Reminders will also be sent via our newsletter and posted periodically on our Facebook and Instagram. Sign up for our newsletter in the footer or from your account dashboard.

What time zone does the event run according to?

#ifchic24 runs from 12:01am ET to 11:59pm ET (Eastern U.S time zone)

How long will this event last?

The event lasts the whole day (24 hours) starting at 12:01am ET and ending at 11:59pm ET on the 24th of every month.
